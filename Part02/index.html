Chicken Tostadas

Want the perfect weeknight dinner? Look no further than a crunchy and savory chicken tostada!
Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup crumbled queso fresco
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Brush both sides of each tortilla with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake tortillas for 10-12 minutes or until they are crispy and golden brown.
  4. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, smoked paprika, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Spread black beans and corn over the tostadas. Add the chicken mixture on top.
  6. Top with diced tomatoes, cilantro, and crumbled queso fresco.
  7. Squeeze lime wedges over the tostadas before serving.
