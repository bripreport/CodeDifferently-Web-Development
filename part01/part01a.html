What is Telugu?

Telugu is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is also spoken in some parts of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and other neighboring states. Telugu is the third most spoken language in India, after Hindi and Bengali. It has a rich history and culture, and Telugu literature dates back to the 11th century AD.

Tollywood is the term used to refer to the Telugu film industry, which is based in Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana. Tollywood is one of the largest film industries in India, producing hundreds of films each year. The industry is known for its unique storytelling style and catchy music. Telugu cinema has produced many talented actors, directors, and technicians, who have made a significant contribution to Indian cinema. Many Telugu films have achieved commercial success and critical acclaim both nationally and internationally.



5. Rangasthalam

Released: March 2018 | Mythri Movie Makers

Rangasthalam is a period action drama directed by Sukumar and stars Ram Charan Konidela and Samantha Akkineni in lead roles. The movie is set in the 1980s and follows the story of a hearing-impaired villager who takes on a powerful landlord to avenge his brother's death. Rangasthalam stands out for its stunning visuals, authentic portrayal of rural life, and strong performances. The movie is known for its emotional depth and showcases the nuances of human relationships in a compelling manner.

4. Khaidi No. 150

Released: January 2017 | Konidela Production Company

Khaidi No. 150 is an action drama directed by V. V. Vinayak and stars megastar Chiranjeevi Konidela in the lead role. The movie follows the story of an escaped prisoner named Kaththi Seenu who becomes entangled in a case of mistaken identity as Shankar, a promiment human rights activist who was secretly killed years ago. While impersonating Shankar for the money it brang him, Seenu realizes that he's fighting for the rights of farmers who are being exploited by an MNC. The rest of the film revolves around his mission to expose the corruption and bring justice to the farmers. Khaidi No. 150 is known for its strong social message, entertaining action sequences, and Chiranjeevi's powerful performance. The movie was a commercial success and marked Chiranjeevi's comeback to the big screen after a 10-year hiatus.

3. Bahubali

Released: March 2015 & April 2017 | Arka Media Works

Bahubali 1 & 2 are epic fantasy movies directed by S. S. Rajamouli and star Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, and Tamannaah in lead roles. The movies follow the story of a warrior who rises to overthrow a ruthless king and claim his rightful place as the ruler of the kingdom. Bahubali stands out for its stunning visual effects, gripping storyline, and strong performances. The movies were a commercial and critical success, and they have raised the bar for Indian cinema Indefinitely.

1. Pushpa: The Rise

Released: December 2021 | Mythri Movie Makers

Pushpa is an action thriller directed by Sukumar and stars Allu Arjun, Rashmika Mandanna, and Fahadh Faasil in lead roles. The movie follows the story of a smuggler named Pushpa who becomes involved in a dangerous game with the police and a rival gang. Pushpa stands out for its stunning cinematography, intense action sequences, and Allu Arjun's powerful performance. The movie also features an intense storyline that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats. The film is acclaimed for Allu Arjun's out-of-the-box gritty and violent role, compared to his usual roles (Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo, etc).
